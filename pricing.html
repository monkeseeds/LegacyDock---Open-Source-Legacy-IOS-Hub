Plan principles

The open-source app stays useful on its own.

Local-first by default

Device data, compatibility checks, snapshots, and exports should work offline in the local app.

Cloud is convenience

Paid cloud plans should add sync, shared history, analytics, and collaboration, not basic device access.

No hidden telemetry

Community intelligence should be opt-in, explainable, and safe for sensitive device histories.
